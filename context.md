# Blackstone Remodeling Services -- Growth Strategy & SEO Plan

## 1. Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)

### Property Manager (On-Site)

-   Manages day-to-day operations of apartment communities
-   Handles tenants, vendors, budgets, and maintenance coordination
-   Success = low vacancy, fast unit turns, minimal complaints
-   Pain: vendors no-show, poor communication, slow repairs

### Maintenance Supervisor

-   Oversees maintenance staff and vendors
-   Focused on execution, safety, and timelines
-   Pain: coordinating multiple vendors, poor workmanship, delays

### Regional / Corporate Manager

-   Oversees multiple properties and budgets
-   Focused on NOI, performance, and scalability
-   Pain: inconsistent vendors, lack of reporting, budget overruns

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Core Pain Points

### Operational

-   Vendors don't show up or are late
-   Poor communication and unclear quotes
-   Slow unit turns
-   Multiple vendors required for one job

### Financial

-   Lost rent from vacancy delays
-   Cost overruns and change orders
-   Repeat repairs

### Emotional / Internal

-   Stress from tenants and corporate pressure
-   Overwork and burnout
-   Fear of looking incompetent
